Clluc is looking for Software Developers

We're creating a team of builders - engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs - who love what we do. Everyone at Clluc is passionate about what they are doing. We have an attitude for doing things well. We are a geek, small and focused team, starting from scratch. If you think this Bitcoin/Blockchain thing might be kind of a big deal, join us :)

Apreciate:

  • Remote friendly
  • Continuous work day in Summer
  • Our offices are located at Serrano, 16, Madrid

We don't evaluate the candidate for years of experience. For us, the most important thing is your contribution to the company, your skills and your attitude. Thus, you will have to complete and defend a coding exercise and answer a few questions about software development.

Scala Software Developer

Must to have:

  • You have a solid knowledge about Java language and JVM.
  • You are concerned about writing clean code and caring of the quality.
  • You are a polyglot programmer and enjoy learning new languages and tools.
  • You are a familiar with concepts like Agile, Devops, Extreme Programming and TDD.
  • You know your way through Linux servers and app deployments.
  • You master git and the Github Workflow.

Nice to have:

  • Demonstrably knowledge in Scala and Akka (Most of our projects are coded in Scala)
  • You know Bitcoin and Ethereum.
  • You have background in mathematics and statistics.
  • Knowledge about Big Data Technologies.

Salary: 35.000€ - 42.000€ /Year

Junior Scala Software Developer

  • If you're passionate about technology and development.
  • You are starting with our stack tech.
  • You stay in line with our work philosophy.
  • And Bitcoin & Ethereum catch your attention.

Just jump in and contact us. We could work in Clluc.
